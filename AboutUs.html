About Us

Welcome to Landmark of Legacy!

Our mission is to foster cultural integration and community unity by celebrating the rich histories and significant landmarks of Kitchener-Waterloo and Brantford.

Explore Local History

Discover local history through our interactive map, which features pins linking to detailed descriptions and images of historic sites. Each pin opens a page with brief, engaging descriptions and high-quality images, making exploration intuitive and enjoyable.

Community Involvement

We value community input. Our Attachment Portal allows you to suggest and recommend historical sites, with all submissions verified by our team.

Our Audience

While our primary audience includes students aged 22 to 40, Landmark of Legacy is designed to be accessible and appealing to anyone interested in local history. Our modern, user-friendly design ensures a captivating experience for all.

Join us in celebrating our shared heritage and contribute to the cultural legacy of our vibrant community.
